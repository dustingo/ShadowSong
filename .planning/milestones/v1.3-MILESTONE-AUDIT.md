## Tech Debt

### Phase 14

- Dedup updates still call `h.db.Save(&existing)` without checking the returned error before continuing.
- Searchability is proven at app-log field level, not against an external log aggregation contract.

### Phase 15

- There is no dedicated regression for the combination of fallback-to-default notification content and repeated transient send failure ending in `terminal_failure`.

### Phase 16

- `channel_lookup` remains less rigorously asserted than the primary failure-path events and still leans on substring checks.

### Phase 17

- The operations runbook assumes Phase 14-16 verification artifacts remain discoverable after future archival moves.
- Runtime naming debt is documented, not resolved: `github.com/game-ops/ai-alert-system` and JWT issuer `ai-alert-system` remain deferred contracts.

## Audit Conclusion

v1.3 is ready for milestone closeout from a requirements and integration standpoint.

Recommended interpretation:

- Safe to archive and tag the milestone.
- Do not treat the milestone as “zero debt.”
- Carry the recorded debt into backlog or next-milestone planning rather than reopening v1.3 scope unless you specifically want a cleanup phase before closeout.
